About me

Tummies and Babies Photography

Hi, I am Olakusibe Animashaun, owner of Tummies and Babies. As a passionate and experienced newborn and maternity photographer, I have been capturing the precious moments of families for over 4 years. With a keen eye for detail and a love for creating beautiful and timeless photographs, I specialize in creating stunning portraits that celebrate the joy and beauty of pregnancy and the innocence of newborns. My journey as a photographer started with a deep fascination for the miracle of life and the power of capturing those fleeting moments that soon become cherished memories. From the glow of an expectant mother to the tiny fingers and toes of a newborn, I focus on the small details that make each moment extraordinary. I look forward to continuing this journey, creating cherished memories and capturing the extraordinary moments that make each family's story truly remarkable.
